Variables de decisión:

  • \(variable1_{i}\): descripción variable 1.

  • \(variableBinaria_{ij}: \begin{cases}1\text{,}&\text{ condición}\text{;}\\ 0\text{,} & \text{ d.l.c.} \end{cases}\)

Modelo:

\[ \text{maximizar } \sum_{k\in K}\sum_{i\in R}p_kx_{ki} \text{ (1)} \]

sujeto a,

\[\begin{split} \begin{align*} x_{ki} &\ge l_{ki}\%\sum_{j\in R} x_{kj}, &&\forall k\in K,i\in R; &(2)\\ x_{ki} &\le u_{ki}\%\sum_{j\in R} x_{kj}, &&\forall k\in K,i\in R; &(3)\\ \sum_{k\in K} x_{ki} &\le b_i, &&\forall i\in R; &(4)\\ \sum_{i\in R} x_{ki} &\ge d_k, &&\forall k\in K; &(5)\\ x_{ki} & \ge 0, &&\forall k\in K,i\in R. &(6) \end{align*} \end{split}\]
